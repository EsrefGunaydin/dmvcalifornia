# Traffic Signs for Turkish Tests

This folder contains traffic sign images used in the Turkish DMV tests.

## Required Images:

### Question 4 - Divided Highway Sign
- **Filename**: `divided-highway.png`
- **Description**: Yellow diamond warning sign showing two lanes merging/dividing
- **Used in**: Turkish Test #2, Question 4

### Question 5 - Handicap Parking Sign
- **Filename**: `handicap-parking.png`
- **Description**: Blue rectangular sign with white wheelchair symbol
- **Used in**: Turkish Test #2, Question 5

### Question 23 - One Way Sign
- **Filename**: `one-way-right.png`
- **Description**: Black and white rectangular sign saying "ONE WAY" with arrow pointing right
- **Used in**: Turkish Test #2, Question 23

### Question 26 - Left Turn or U-Turn Allowed
- **Filename**: `left-turn-u-turn.png`
- **Description**: White square sign with black curved arrow showing left turn/U-turn permitted
- **Used in**: Turkish Test #2, Question 26

## Image Specifications:
- Format: PNG (preferred) or JPG
- Recommended size: 300x300 to 500x500 pixels
- Background: Transparent (for PNG) or white
- Quality: High resolution for clarity

## Adding Images:
Place the actual traffic sign images in this folder with the filenames listed above.
